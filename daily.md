## üåê **GitHubBox: Live Preview for GitHub Projects**

### ‚úÖ **What is GitHubBox?**

**[GitHubBox](https://githubbox.com)** is a tool to **instantly preview HTML/CSS/JS projects from GitHub** in your browser without downloading or cloning. Perfect for frontend developers!

---

### üí° **Why Use It?**

* üîé Preview HTML/CSS/JS directly from GitHub
* üöÄ No need to clone/download the repo
* üì§ Great for sharing frontend projects with mentors, friends, or online

---

### üõ†Ô∏è **How to Use GitHubBox**

#### üîπ **Method 1: Basic GitHubBox Preview**

1. Copy your GitHub file URL:
   üëâ `https://github.com/username/repo/blob/main/index.html`

2. Replace `github.com` with `githubbox.com`:
   ‚úÖ `https://githubbox.com/username/repo/blob/main/index.html`

3. Open this in your browser ‚Äî done!

---

#### üîπ **Method 2: Lightweight HTML Preview (htmlpreview\.github.io)**

1. Get the **raw URL** of your HTML file:
   üëâ `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/username/repo/main/index.html`

2. Paste it after:
   ‚úÖ `https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://raw.githubusercontent.com/username/repo/main/index.html`

3. Open the link in your browser to preview.

---
